Spicy Pumpkin Curry

Description

I love curry. Especially those spicy Indian ones. You could feed those to me anyday. If the same goes for you, you'll love this dish!

Especially in fall when pumpkins are everywhere this is a proper seasonal dish, and veggie too! It goes perfectly with rice as a safe option, but I can recommend trying it with naan as well.

Ingredients for 4 people

  • 400gr chopped pumpkin
  • 250gr passata
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tsp ginger paste
  • 240gr chickpeas
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 or 2 tsp chili
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon garam massala
  • 70gr tomato paste
  • 50gr butter
  • 2 tablespoon (olive) oil

Steps

  1. Boil the pumpkin in plenty water for 10 minutes or until soft
  2. Chop the onion and fry for about 5 minutes
  3. Press and add the garlic and the ginger paste and fry for 2-3 more minutes
  4. Add the cinnamon, turmeric, chili, cumin, salt and garam massala and fry 1 more minute
  5. Add the tomato paste fry shortly
  6. Add the chickpeas and stir thoroughly to mix with the spices. Fry for a few minutes
  7. Now add the coconut milk and passata and stir until a creamy sauce forms
  8. Add the boiled pumpkin and the butter and let it simmer gently for 5 minutes.
  9. Your curry is done! Serve with rice or naan.
